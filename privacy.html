1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, and optional profile photo.
  • Expense Data: Details of expenses you enter (amounts, notes, group members).
  • Chat Data: Messages shared within groups or friends list.
  • Device Information: Basic device details to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve YoyoWash services.
  • To send you notifications about updates, settlements, and reminders.
  • To maintain a safe and user-friendly environment.

3. Sharing of Information

  • We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties.
  • Data is shared only within your group or friends in the app, as intended by the features.

4. Data Security

  • We use secure storage and encryption to protect your data.
  • While we strive to safeguard your data, no system is 100% secure.

5. User Control

  • You can edit or delete your expenses and personal data anytime within the app.
  • If you wish to permanently delete your account, please contact us.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • YoyoWash is not intended for children under 13.
  • If we learn that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally.
  • You will be notified of major changes through the app.
